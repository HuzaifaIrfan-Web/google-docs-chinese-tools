# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

## Overview

Chinese Tools for Google Docs ("the Add-on") is designed to help users format Chinese text by applying tone colors and adding Zhuyin or Pinyin annotations.

Your privacy is important. The Add-on does not collect, store, or share your personal information.

## Information We Access

The Add-on only accesses the currently opened Google Docs document to perform actions requested by the user, such as:

- Apply tone colors to Chinese characters
- Add Zhuyin (Bopomofo) annotations
- Add Pinyin annotations
- Update or remove formatting

The document content is processed only to perform these requested operations.

## Data Collection

The Add-on does NOT collect:

- Personal information
- Email addresses
- Google account information
- Document history
- Analytics
- Usage statistics
- Advertising identifiers

## Data Storage

No document content or personal information is stored on external servers.

Any preferences (such as color presets or settings) are stored using Google's Apps Script Properties Service associated with your account or document, when applicable.

## Third-Party Services

The Add-on does not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.

If optional online dictionary or pronunciation services are added in future versions, they will be clearly disclosed.

## Security

The Add-on only requests the permissions necessary to modify the current Google Docs document and save user preferences.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the Add-on evolves. Changes will be published in this repository.
